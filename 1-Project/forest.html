Why It Matters

Natural landscapes provide us with clean air, fresh water, and a home for countless species. It's our duty to protect them for future generations.
Preserving Earth's landscapes involves various actions to protect natural and cultural elements, including natural areas of beauty, and everyday or degraded landscapes. It's a broad concept encompassing conservation, land restoration, and sustainable land management practices

Major Threats to Our Landscapes

Deforestation

Forests are vanishing due to logging and farming.

Climate Change

Rising temperatures and natural disasters are altering landscapes.

Urbanization

Expanding cities are eating into natural spaces.

Pollution

Plastic and chemicals are destroying ecosystems.
